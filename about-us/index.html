PNHS WebSibol — The website initiative of Padada National High School

PNHS WebSibol

The Official Website of Padada National High School

Rationale

PNHS WebSibol establishes an official, structured school website that goes beyond the limits of social media. It centralizes announcements, learning resources, and issuances; improves communication with students and parents; and strengthens the school’s professional digital presence.

Objectives

  • Provide a centralized platform for school information and resources.
  • Improve communication between the school, students, and parents.
  • Enhance learning through accessible online materials.
